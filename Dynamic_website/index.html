Our Story

The name of the restaurant, Tryzub, means trident in Ukrainian, and it is a symbol of national identity for all Ukrainians. Millions of Ukrainians have died throughout the centuries in the fight for freedom and independence that this symbol signifies. The largest tryzub in the world is outside the restaurant in the four story brickwork of the building that the restaurant occupies.

Menus

Situated in the heart of Ukrainian Village in Chicago, this trend setting restaurant and bar showcases traditional Ukrainian fare with a modern twist. Try our multi-colored Varenyky (Pierogi), each color shell infused with a natural vegetable juice or spice and with its own unique filling - and all handmade daily in our kitchen. You'll love the crispy crunchy Potato Pancakes, and our umami Carpathian Wild Mushroom soup is made with authentic mountain grown Ukrainian white mushrooms. Don't forget to follow it up with a few of the dozens of flavors of our house-infused spirits (nastoyanky)!
